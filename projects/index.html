Building the first public dataset of group homes in the US

This research project started as a result of spending hundreds of hours on the phone trying to find a home for someone suffering from severe schizophrenia and no income or insurance, and realizing how difficult it was to get a simple list of where Medicaid would pay for their housing. This fact – that we couldn’t access a comprehensive list of group homes or assisted living facilities for someone in need – made us realize that we shouldn’t gaslight ourselves....
